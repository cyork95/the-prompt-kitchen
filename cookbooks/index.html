📚 Cookbooks

Curated prompt collections built around a specific kind of person — and the specific problems they actually face.

The Recipe Book has great recipes for everyone. A Cookbook goes further — it's a curated collection built around your situation. The Indie Hacker Cookbook and the Student Cookbook both cover "how to get feedback on your writing," but they're asking for very different things. These cookbooks speak your language and skip the irrelevant parts.

📖 Recipe Book

Universal patterns that work for anyone. Good when you know the technique you need.

📚 Cookbook

Audience-specific collections. Good when you know your situation and want everything relevant in one place.

🌶️ Spice Rack

Interactive prompt builder. Good when you want a ready-to-paste prompt with your details filled in.

🥫 The Pantry

Reusable building blocks. Good when you want to customize or compose your own prompts.
