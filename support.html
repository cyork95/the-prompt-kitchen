Support the Kitchen

The Prompt Kitchen is free. No ads, no sponsored content, no email list, no paywalled recipes. Everything on this site is written to be genuinely useful — not to generate clicks or sell you something.

It takes real time to research, write, and maintain. If something here has saved you an hour, helped you write something better, or made AI feel less intimidating, I'd be grateful for your support. Anything helps, and nothing is expected.

Donations go toward hosting, domain, time spent writing new content, and — honestly — the coffee that makes it all happen.

Other ways to help — all free

🔗

Share a page that helped you

The single most impactful thing you can do. Copy a link to any page and share it with someone who would find it useful. Word of mouth is how small independent sites survive.

🐙

Contribute on GitHub

Found a mistake? Have a prompt pattern that's not here? Want to add gallery examples? The site is open source. See CONTRIBUTING.md for how to submit improvements — no coding experience required for content changes.

✉️

Send feedback or ideas

Have a suggestion, spotted a bug, or want to pitch a new recipe? Reach out at promptkitchen@yorkdevelops.com or open a GitHub issue. What's confusing? What's missing? Which recipes didn't work the way you expected? Honest feedback makes the site genuinely better.
