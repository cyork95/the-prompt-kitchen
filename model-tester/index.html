🧪 Test Kitchen

Tell us what you're trying to do. We'll recommend the three best free AIs for the job, then help you synthesize all three answers into one final result using a judge AI.

💡 How this works click to expand

Different AIs have different strengths. Instead of guessing which one to use, run your prompt through three well-matched models and then use a fourth to synthesize the best final answer — all with free-tier tools, no accounts required for most.
