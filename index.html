The Prompt Kitchen way

This is genuinely simple

You don't need an account here, or a technical background. Just follow the recipe.

1

Pick a recipe

Browse our library of prompt patterns organized by what you're trying to do — writing, research, learning, creating.

2

Copy the prompt

Every recipe has a "Copy" button. Click it and the prompt goes straight to your clipboard, ready to use.

3

Paste into any AI

Open ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any other AI tool you like. Paste your prompt and get your result.

4

Tweak & repeat

Our tips help you adjust the recipe to your situation. The more specific you are, the better your results.

Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, Copilot, and any other chat-based AI.

Featured recipes

Start with these crowd-pleasers

These prompt patterns get used every day by people of all skill levels.

Learning

Teach Me Like I'm [level]

Get any concept explained at exactly the right level — from "explain it to a 10-year-old" to "assume I have a PhD."
Writing

Draft → Critique → Revise Loop

Stop settling for the first draft. Use this three-step loop to get writing that's genuinely better, not just longer.
Planning

Research → Plan → Implement

The go-to pattern for tackling anything complex. AI helps you understand the problem before jumping to solutions.
Daily Use

Email Tone Adjustment

Paste your rough draft and ask AI to make it warmer, more professional, or shorter. Works every time.
How-To

Step-by-Step Breakdown

Turn any overwhelming task into a clear, numbered list of manageable steps. Perfect for new projects.

Rubber Duck Debugging

Explain your problem out loud to the AI. Half the time, you'll realize the answer before it even responds.

Our promise to you

No jargon. No hype. No tricks.

We wrote every word on this site asking one question: "Would my parents understand this on first read?" If the answer was no, we rewrote it. No buzzwords, no "leverage AI to unlock synergies" — just practical, honest guidance about tools that are genuinely useful if you know how to use them.
