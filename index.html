The Prompt Kitchen way

This is genuinely simple

You don't need an account here, or a technical background. Just follow the recipe.

1

Pick a recipe

Browse the Recipe Book for prompt patterns organized by what you're trying to do — writing, research, learning, creating.

2

Copy the prompt

Every recipe has a Copy button. Click it and the prompt goes straight to your clipboard, ready to use.

3

Paste into any AI

Open Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any tool you like. Paste your prompt and get your result.

4

Tweak & repeat

Our tips help you adjust the recipe to your situation. The more specific you are, the better the results.

Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, Copilot, Perplexity, DeepSeek, and any other chat-based AI.

Our promise to you

No jargon. No hype. No tricks.

We wrote every word on this site asking one question: "Would my parents understand this on first read?" If the answer was no, we rewrote it. No buzzwords, no "leverage AI to unlock synergies" — just practical, honest guidance about tools that are genuinely useful if you know how to use them.

This site is free, has no ads, no tracking, and no cookies. It's a cookbook, not a product. If it helps you, consider supporting the kitchen.
